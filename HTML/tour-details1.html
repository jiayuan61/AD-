Spain (Marbella, Costa Del Sol)

2 Weeks Get ready for the most educational and exciting summer camp of all time.

Marbella Summer Camp


GCSE / A/S and A2-Level students from the age of 14-18years old
Start: 25 June 2017*
*Starting date is tentative. Send us a message or chat with us to let us know more about your preferred start date.

Click here if you interested to join our University Student Internship program.

Join our summer camp and we assure we link you up with the best teachers and provide for you an interesting line up of activities and of course, great mouth-watering food!

In order to ensure the best learning environment for our students, lessons will be conducted in hotels with breathtaking scenery. Activites will also be arranged with only the best vendors in Costa Del Sol.

We understand the importance of parents spending quality time with their children especially during summer holiday. Hence, we welcome all parents to join us in this camp as well! We have an itinerary just for you as we believe that this educational trip cannot be better than seeing a family together and promoting family bonding as well!



Education

Spanish, Chinese or English lessons
Lesson duration 45 minutes
One language up to 15 per week
Two Languages up to 15 per week
Three Languages 5 + 5 + 5 per week

There will be a maximum of 8 students in each class and classes are organised according to the standards of students.

Academics
Lesson duration 1 hour
GCSE /'A' levels / IB
Math up to 15* per week
Science(Chemistry, Biology & Physics) up to 15* per week
Business up to 15* per week
ICT up to 15* per week
Humanities up to 15* per week

*The number of lessons are tentative as we understand that different students do different subjects in school and there are some who might want to focus on certain subjects. Hence, before the start of the summer camp, we will customise the best schedule for the students.

It is also possible to arrange for a one-to-one class with the teachers depending on their schedule. Additional charges may apply

Activites

Sports

We offer a series of exciting sports activities for both students and parents.

Golf lessons and tournament - Play in our trusted partner's Golf Clubs such as Sotogrande. Learn from the best and talented coaches who have played in US Open and PGA from Michael Campbell Golf Academy.

Horse Riding - Horse riding session with our partner, Endurance Equestrian and Escuela De Arte Ecuestre

Rugby - Learn Rugby with Marbella Rugby

Football - Join football training camp with Marbella Football Center

Footgolf


Football + Golf ? Learn how to play this very unique sport with our partner, FootGolf Marbell.

Kite Surfing - Go kite suring with our trusted partners, Sotogrande Watersports, Freedom Kite School and High Flyers Tarifa.

Kayaking

Paddle Boarding - Paddle on flat water and ride the wave with Stand Up Paddle

Biking Trails - We can do guided mountain bike trips with our partners, Hike and Bike and Costa Cycling Experience

Canyoning - Descend natural canyons and learn mountaineering techniques, abseiling, ropework and some swimming with our trusted partner, Paddle in Spain

Climbing - Attend climbing courses in one of the biggest climbing area in Europe at El Chorro with our trusted partner, Climbing Lodge

Fencing - With Olympian Eva who is 3rd in the World in team & 5 European Championship

Sailing - Learn sailing with our trusted partners in Estepona, Marbell and Sotogrande

Polo - Discover the world of polo at Santa Maria Polo Club which is one of the best polo clubs in the world that organise more than 25 tournaments per year. At the same time, learn more about polo playing from the best players.

Aquasports / Gym - We can offer together with Glow Wellness and Fitness aquasports such as aquagym and gym activities such as circuit training.

Diving* - Start learning how to dive with our trusted partner, Happy Divers Marbella.

*Happy Divers Marbella conducts class for students age 8 and above.


Leisure

We offer a series of fun activities that both student and parent can enjoy.

Buggy + Jeep Tour - Ride through the Estepona Mountains in buggies with our trusted partner, Sol Buggys


Ballooning - Get the best view of Andalucia on a hotair balloon.


Girocopter - Go on a Girocopter and fly over Costa Del Sol with our partner, Experience Box Spain

Yacht / Dolphin Sighting Boat Trip - Go on Yacht trips with our partner, Yachting Sotogrande.

Party Boat - We can arrange the ultimate social event and entertainment on a boat for everyone in the tour with Marbs Parties.

Team building / Bonding activities - We also offer team building activities with our partners, Experience Box Spain,

Workshop We can organise interesting workshops such as Lifestyle and Film Making

Sight-seeing - We can arrange trips to Bioparc, Butterfly Park, Chocolate Factory, Sea Life, Banal Madena and Selwo Marina Delfinarium.


Flamenco / Salsa classes

Water Park - Trips to water parks such as Sould Park and Aquavelis can be arranged as well.

Amusement Park - We can arrange trips to one of the most popular amusement park, Tivoli World.

Gastronomy & Cooking Classes - Learn, Taste and Discover the different food in Spain with our partner, Experience Box Spain.

Paraglide / Paramotor - Learn how to Paraglide or Paramotor with our trusted partner, Paisajes Aereos.



Accomodation

In order to ensure that our students have the most comfortable accomodation, rest assured that we will only choose the best in the region. We also guarantee amazing views along with conducive environments and facilities to conduct lessons.
