We are hiring!

By: Alpha Dragon Travel Team Posted: 27 Oct 2016

Want to travel and teach?


Internship Program

Alpha Dragon Travel is currently looking for experienced teachers, university undergraduates or graduates who can teach the following subjects:

Chinese Language

Mathematics up to A level standard

Science*(Chemistry, Physics & Biology) preferably up to A level standard

Able to conduct some** of our listed culture classes.

*Any 2 combinations are sufficient.

**The list is not exhaustive. Hence, if you have any good suggestions please indicate that in your resume.

Qualities we are looking out for among potential teachers: We want our teachers to not just be good educators, but also serve as good role models to our students with excellent manners and values. It will be a bonus if the teacher has a wide range of interests which can help to enhance the variety of our classes. Also, this role will be best suited for someone who has a strong spirit of adventure and adapts to a new culture quickly.

You will be expected to teach for at least 4 hours a day and for the rest of the time you will join the students in the activities that are organised.

You will be expected to do some lesson planning before the start of the trip which includes printing course materials for the students.

For university students or graduates who has experience in teaching and fit the above criteria, you are welcome to join. At the moment, we would not be offering remuneration for your internship. However, we promise you that this will definitely be a rewarding experience for you as you embark on an unique and interesting adventure.

We promise you that you will have your share of fun as well! You will enjoy free accomodation in the best hotels, best dining experiences and of course, participate in the activities we have planned!

Through this internship, you will get the amazing opportunity to travel to 4 countries

Spain

France

Germany

Austria

We will fly you to Spain and back from where the summer camp ends. Also, food and accomodation will be included in this program.

We will prefer interns who can stay with us from the start of our summer camp in Spain till the end.

Duration: 27 June to 29 July 2017

So what are you waiting for? Apply right now if you have what it takes to be part of our exciting summer camps!
